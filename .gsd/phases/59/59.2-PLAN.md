# Plan 59.2: Replicar Pipeline Kanban CRM en Proyectos

## Objective

## Context
- **CRM Standard (Fondo Columnas Pipeline)**:
  Contenedor de columna en `CRMModule.jsx`:
  `bg-[var(--color-surface-variant)]/40 border border-[var(--color-outline-variant)] rounded-2xl h-full min-h-[300px]`
- **CRM Standard (Tarjeta / Pastilla con Franja)**:

  `bg-[var(--color-surface-container)] border border-[var(--color-outline-variant)] p-4 rounded-xl shadow-sm hover:border-[#6B4FD8]/50 transition-all cursor-pointer group relative overflow-hidden`
  Franja Lateral: 
  `<div className={\`absolute top-0 left-0 w-1 h-full opacity-20 \${stage.color}\`}></div>`

- **Targets**: `ProjectKanban.jsx`
